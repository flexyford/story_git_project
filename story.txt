[1]   It was a windy day.
      
      The mailman barely made it to the front door. When the door opened, Mrs Penninton said, "hello", but before she had a real chance to say "thank you", the mail blew out of the mailman's hands, into the house and the front door sammed in his face. Mrs. Pennington ran to pick up the mail.

      "Oh my," she said.

[16]  "There," she said, "it will be safe until we get home."

      Tommy smiled, ran into the car and got in. He rolled down the back window and looked up into the sky. He wondered where the old lead had gone. Perhaps one day he would see what the old leaf had seen - perhaps.
